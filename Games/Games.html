Star Wars Battlefront 2D

Star Wars Battlefront 2D is a game based on Star Wars Battlefield 2. The player creates their own levels and then plays against the AI. Multiplayer is not finished and is disabled in this version. Star Wars Battlefront 2D was started by Skyler Burwell in 2009 when he was a sophomore in highschool.

Download the pre-alpha for Windows.
